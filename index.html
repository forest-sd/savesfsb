Why Small Businesses Matter

Small businesses account for over 90% of San Francisco's businesses and employ hundreds of thousands of residents. They define the character of neighborhoods â€” from the bakeries of the Sunset to the bookshops of North Beach to the boutiques of Hayes Valley.
